About Our System

Our intuitive car rental system puts you in control:

  • Smart Vehicle Browse - View available cars sorted by make, with clear reservation status indicators
  • Flexible Booking - Choose preset durations (hour/day/week) or custom rentals (with adjusted pricing)
  • Dual Payment Options - Pay directly by card or use account balance for loyalty rewards
  • Instant Cost Calculation - System automatically computes rental totals including any applicable fees
  • Credit & Loyalty Benefits - Our private credit system boosts your standing with each rental
  • Secure Accounts - Simple email/password registration with validation checks for security

The command-line interface ensures fast, reliable service for both new and experienced users.
